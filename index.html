ABOUT

Capture the Flag is a security hackathon - which means you’ll actually be hacking! Participants compete against other student teams. The event is open to students of all skill levels and we are especially beginner friendly. The goal is to capture a flag hidden in the solution of a challenging question. The more difficult a question, the more points your team is awarded for "capturing" that particular flag.

FAQ

How many people can be on a team?

The character End of Transmission (EoT), 0x04 (that means four people)

What topics does California CTF cover?

Cryptography, Web Hacking, Steganography, Forensics and Reverse Engineering among others

The only experience with hacking I have is watching Mission Impossible. Where should I start?

  • Hacksplaining - General hacking lessons & exercises
  • SecLists - The security tester's companion - a collection of multiple types of lists used during security assessments
  • SQLBolt - SQL Brushup
  • Learn X in Y Minutes - Learning Python
